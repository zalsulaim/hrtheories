About & Mission

A firm defined by its commitments.

HR Theories is a boutique executive search firm based in Riyadh, serving the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the wider GCC. We place senior leaders across the region's critical sectors — with the discretion, rigour, and accountability that decisive appointments demand. Our purpose is best expressed as a set of commitments to those we serve.

To our Clients

HR Theories is committed to the placement of leaders who endure. Through a sourcing reach exceeding 200 million professional profiles, rigorous assessment-informed evaluation, absolute confidentiality, and a written replacement guarantee, we deliver appointments of lasting value. We provide a candid assessment of every mandate, respond within one business day, and measure our success by the tenure and contribution of those we place.

To our Candidates

We are committed to representing every professional with discretion, accuracy, and respect. We make contact only where a role genuinely aligns with a candidate's profile, safeguard all personal data, and provide honest counsel throughout the process. We regard each placement as the foundation of an enduring professional relationship.

To our Partners & Employees

We are committed to fairness, transparency, and mutual growth. We invest in the development of our people, honour our obligations to our partners, and hold our organisation to the same standards of merit and assessment that we apply on behalf of our clients.

To our Environment

We are committed to operating ethically and in full alignment with the laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, including data-protection, labour, and Saudization requirements. We conduct every engagement with integrity and in support of the national objectives of Vision 2030.
