Why the right leader matters.

A single senior appointment shapes strategy, culture, and results for years. The cost of getting it wrong — in disruption, lost momentum, and a repeated search — dwarfs any fee. Executive search exists to remove that risk: to reach the leaders who are not applying, evaluate them properly, and stand behind the decision. That is the whole of what we do.

Our process.

01

Research & market-map

02

Questions.

What makes HR Theories different from a large search firm?

We are a boutique built for senior appointments, not volume. That means direct partner-level attention, complete discretion, an assessment-led approach to reducing hiring risk, and a replacement guarantee that extends further than the market standard — combined with a sourcing reach that rivals the largest firms.

How does the extended guarantee work?

Every retained search includes a free-replacement guarantee. When our assessment tools inform the hire, that guarantee is extendable from 3 up to 11 months, scaling with the rigour of the assessment applied to the shortlist. Full terms are set out in the engagement letter; conditions apply.

Do you support Saudization and Vision 2030 leadership hiring?

Yes. We specialise in placing senior leaders across the Kingdom's key sectors, and we operate in full alignment with Saudization requirements and the national ambition of Vision 2030 to build world-class Saudi leadership.

What sectors do you cover?

Our focus sectors are Industrial & Manufacturing, Energy, Utilities & Telecom, Government & Semi-Government, Corporate Functions, and Banking & Insurance. Our 200M+ reach means we can search effectively well beyond these when a mandate calls for it.

How fast will I see a shortlist?

We respond to every brief within one business day and move quickly from there. Because we start from the whole market rather than a static database, credible shortlists come together faster than a traditional search — without cutting corners on evaluation.

Is assessment mandatory?

No. Executive search is our core service and stands on its own. Assessment is an option — recommended for the appointments where a mistake is most costly — and choosing it is what extends your replacement guarantee.
